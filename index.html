THE STORY

"Your journey may be tough, but remember — you too can come back and build a road to success."

In 2019, a catastrophic stroke left Miles Prather paralyzed on his right side at just 20 years old. The journey to recovery has been one of the toughest roads imaginable — but Miles refused to be defined by his struggles.

Through determination and the support of his loving family, he forged ahead and founded Tuff Road Rugs — a testament that even in the face of the toughest challenges, we can rebuild our lives and create something extraordinary.

— Miles Prather, founder & artist
